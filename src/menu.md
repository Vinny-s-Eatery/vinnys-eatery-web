# Menu

---

### 1. Beef Noodle (Phở Bò)
Rice noodle, rare beef, brisket beef, onion, spring onion, coriander, bean shoot, Vietnamese mint, chili, lemon with beef broth.

### 2. Spicy Beef Noodle Soup (Bún Bò Huế)
Big round noodle, rare beef, brisket beef, pork, onion, spring onion, coriander, bean shoot, Vietnamese mint, chili, lemon with spicy beef broth.

### 3. Vermicelli Salad with Pork (Heo Xã)
Rice vermicelli, mixed salad, pickled carrot, cucumber, cooked with pork, lemongrass, onion in special sauce. Topped with fried onion, sesame, coriander, chili, and fish sauce.

### 4. Vietnamese Chicken Coleslaw (Gỏi Gà)
Fresh white cabbage, purple cabbage, mixed salad, onion, pickled carrot, cucumber, coriander, fried onion, sesame, bean shoot, chili, cooked breast chicken mixed together in special salad sauce.

### 5. Singapore Noodle (Seafood, Chicken, or Tofu)
Thin rice noodle cooked with onion, egg, chicken, fresh carrot, bean shoot, spring onion with special ingredients topped with coriander, sesame, chili, fried onion.

### 6. Chicken Curry (Cà Ri Gà)
Fresh chicken breast cooked with onion, lemongrass, coconut milk, coconut cream, combined with carrot, potato, special curry ingredients. Served with rice.

### 7. Broken Rice (Cơm Tấm)
Pork chop, fried egg, shredded pork, tomato, pickled carrot. Served with broken rice.

### 8. Crispy Chicken with Red Rice (Cơm Gà Đỏ)
Chicken drumstick deep-fried until crispy, served with tomato, cucumber, mixed salad, special cooked red rice topped with sweet chili sauce and fish sauce.

### 9. BBQ Pork Bánh Mì
Bánh mì filled with chicken pate, BBQ pork, pickled carrot, white and red cabbage, cucumber, coriander, spring onion, fried onion, topped with house sauce, mayo, and chili.

### 10. Bacon and Egg Roll with Small Coffee
Two fried eggs and bacon in buttered bánh mì or toast, topped with salt, pepper, tomato, or BBQ sauce.

### 11. Chicken Pho (Phở Gà)
Rice noodle, chicken, onion, spring onion, coriander, bean shoot, Vietnamese mint, chili, lemon with chicken broth.

### 12. Chicken Wonton Noodle
Egg noodle, BBQ chicken slice, chicken wonton, carrot, bok choy, Chinese cabbage, onion, spring onion, coriander, fried onion, lemon, and chili with chicken broth.

### 13. Vermicelli Salad with Beef (Bò Xã)
Rice vermicelli, mixed salad, pickled carrot, cucumber, cooked with beef, lemongrass, onion in special sauce. Topped with fried onion, sesame, coriander, chili, and fish sauce.

### 14. Vermicelli Salad with Chicken (Gà Xã)
Rice vermicelli, mixed salad, pickled carrot, cucumber, cooked with chicken, lemongrass, onion in special sauce. Topped with fried onion, sesame, coriander, chili, and fish sauce.

### 15. Vermicelli Salad with Tofu (Bún Đậu Hũ)
Rice vermicelli, mixed salad, pickled carrot, cucumber, cooked with tofu, lemongrass, onion in special sauce. Topped with fried onion, sesame, coriander, chili, and soy sauce.

### 16. Chicken Salad Bowl
Chicken, mixed salad, cucumber, shredded carrot, onion, coriander, fried onion, bean shoot, tomato mixed together in special sauce.

### 17. Pork Chop with Rice
Pork chop, tomato, pickled carrot, and mixed salad. Served with rice.

### 18. Plain Fried Rice
Plain rice fried with onion, egg, mixed vegetables, bean shoot, spring onion, topped with coriander, fried onion, sesame, and chili.

### 19. BBQ Chicken Fried Rice
Plain rice fried with BBQ chicken, onion, egg, mixed vegetables, bean shoot, spring onion, topped with coriander, fried onion, sesame, and chili.

### 20. BBQ Pork Fried Rice
Plain rice fried with BBQ pork, onion, egg, mixed vegetables, bean shoot, spring onion, topped with coriander, fried onion, sesame, and chili.

### 21. Tofu Fried Rice (V)
Plain rice fried with tofu, onion, egg, mixed vegetables, bean shoot, spring onion, topped with coriander, fried onion, sesame, and chili.

### 22. Vinny's Special Chicken with Salad
